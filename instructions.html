Instructions

The Meal Prepper™ will create a personalized healthy meal plan for user based on any dietary restrictions and the food they have in their fridge and pantry.

1. First we would like to gather what items the user already has. Go through each page(Protein, Grains, Dairy, Fruit, Vegetables) and select all the items you have.

2. If you have any food restrictions be sure to click on the ones you have.

3. Generate your meal plan for the week.

4. Don't forget to add your name at the top of the page.
